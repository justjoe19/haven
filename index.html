The Studio

Design with
intention
and craft.

Haven was founded on a single belief: that a well-designed space is a form of quiet generosity. We don't impose style — we reveal the essential character of a place and the people who inhabit it.

Every project begins with listening. We study how light moves through your space across a full day. We observe how you move, gather, rest. Only then do we design.

Eleanor Marsh
Founder & Principal Designer

How We Work

A process built
on trust.

Discovery

Deep-listening sessions, site visits, and research into your life, your aspirations, and your architecture.

Concept

Mood boards, material palettes, and spatial narratives that establish the soul of the project.

Design

Detailed drawings, specifications, and coordination with architects, contractors, and artisans.

Realisation

On-site oversight, final styling, and the moment we hand the keys to a space that exceeds expectations.
